About Event

The first gaming tournament co-operate between the two computing clubs, Computer Science Students Club (CSSC) and University Computer Club (UCC), within the top Uni in WA, the University of Western Australia (UWA), making a tournament from the most intensive, most intelligent required Multiplayer Online Battle Arena (MOBA) game.
Defence in the Uni will let every student in interest of Defendence of the Ancient 2 (Dota 2) to have their oppotunity to face against any other students having the same interest and dream.

(Students only event, Current Valid student card needs to present for validation.)

Computer Science Students Club

Frequently Asked Questions

When would the game started?

We decided the game will start during the study break of university semesters To make sure people still have some time for revision, we choose the first 4-8 November to go.

Which payment methods do you accept?

Currently we ask for preferences and we only ask payments after RSVP period. Payment will accept cash only, we will ask for you to pay face to face.

What would happen when I don't want to play anymore?

When we settled and ask for payment, you can choose not to pay. But keep in mind that if you declined your match after you paid, We will eliminate you team and no refund.

What will be the Reward?

Prices

Due to the university policy and the taxation policy, we cannot offer direct cash as reward.

Therefore we will use price, such as steam gift card, electronic products or coupons.
